About ClassKart

Empowering Learning Through Access, Innovation & Community

Our Mission

ClassKart is dedicated to making educational resources accessible, affordable, and of the highest quality for every learner. We believe that education should never be limited by cost or availability, and we're committed to breaking down these barriers one student at a time.

Our Story

Where It All Began

Founded in 2025 by Alice and Fidele, ClassKart was born from a shared vision and personal experience. As students ourselves, we understood the struggle of finding quality educational materials at affordable prices.

What started as a simple idea has grown into a thriving platform serving thousands of students. Today, ClassKart stands as a testament to our commitment to educational excellence and accessibility for all.

Our Core Values

Quality First

We carefully curate every product to ensure learners receive only the best materials that truly support their educational journey.

Accessibility

Education should be available to everyone. We work tirelessly to keep prices fair and resources within reach.

Trust & Support

Your success is our success. We're here to support you every step of the way with reliable service and genuine care.

Growth & Learning

We believe in continuous improvement. As our community grows, so does our commitment to serving you better.
