About ClassKart

Empowering Learning Through Access

Our Mission

At ClassKart, we believe that quality education starts with access to the right materials. Our mission is to empower students, teachers, and parents by providing easy access to affordable, high-quality educational resources that inspire learning and foster growth.

Our Story

Where It All Began

ClassKart was founded in 2025 by Alice and Fidele, two passionate educators who recognized a critical gap in accessing educational materials. They witnessed firsthand how students struggled to find quality resources at affordable prices.

What started as a small initiative to help local students has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of learners across the region. Today, ClassKart stands as a beacon of educational accessibility and excellence.
